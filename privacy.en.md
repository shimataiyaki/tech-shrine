# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy sets forth the handling of data, including personal information, concerning the operation of the "tech-shrine series" (hereinafter referred to as the "Product") provided by Shimataiyaki (hereinafter referred to as the "Developer") to the Information Processing Club (hereinafter referred to as the "User").

## 1. Information Collected
Through the standard operation of the Product, the following information will be recorded automatically or incidentally:
- **digital-ema**: Nicknames, the content of wishes, and the date and time of posts entered by visitors.
- **binary-omikuji**: Logs of lottery results and the number of times displayed.

## 2. Responsibility of the User
1. The User shall be responsible for appropriately informing visitors, either orally or through notices, that their wishes will be displayed on the exhibition monitors and that this Product is an unofficial club exhibition of the school.
2. In providing the collected information to the Developer, the User shall, at its own responsibility, determine the legitimacy of providing such information, including whether necessary consent has been obtained from visitors.

## 3. Purpose of Use of Data by the Developer
The Developer shall use the data provided by the User solely for the following purposes:
1. For the proper operation of the Product during the cultural festival exhibition.
2. For personal inquiry (research) activities to be conducted after the conclusion of the exhibition as part of the **tech-shrine project** (analysis of the quality of experience, verification of social impact in public spaces).

## 4. Data Management and Destruction
1. The Developer shall strictly manage raw data, except when the collected data is processed into a statistical format that does not identify individuals.
2. The Developer shall promptly and completely destroy the raw data as soon as the aforementioned inquiry activities are completed.

## 5. Prohibition of Third-Party Provision
The Developer shall not provide the collected data to any third party, except as required by laws and regulations.

## 6. Right of Judgment and Termination
The User retains the final right of judgment regarding data analysis. However, if a clear objection is raised by a faculty advisor from the perspective of school management, the Developer shall follow such instructions, cease data analysis, and fulfill the obligation to destroy the data.

## 7. Disclaimer
The Developer shall not be liable for any trouble or disputes that may arise between the User and visitors regarding the operation of this Product.
